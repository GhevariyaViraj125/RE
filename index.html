Frequently Asked Questions

What is the minimum down payment to finance a Royal Enfield? +
The minimum down payment varies by model — starting at ₹4,999 for the Hunter 350, ₹9,999 for the Bullet 350, ₹10,999 for the Classic 350 and ₹11,999 for the Meteor 350. The actual down payment is the difference between the on-road price and the loan amount sanctioned by the bank.
What is the maximum loan amount I can avail? +
Our banking partners typically finance up to 90–95% of the on-road price of the motorcycle. Some customers with excellent credit profiles may be eligible for 100% on-road funding. The exact amount depends on your income, credit score, and the lending bank's criteria.
What are the interest rates offered on Royal Enfield finance? +
Interest rates generally range from 9% to 17% per annum, depending on the lending institution, your credit score, loan tenure, and employment type. Salaried individuals with a high CIBIL score often receive the most competitive rates.
How long does the loan approval process take? +
Once you submit your complete application and documents at the dealership, most loan approvals are processed within 24 to 48 hours. In some cases with pre-approved offers from partner banks, same-day approvals are possible.
Can I apply for finance if I am self-employed? +
Yes, absolutely. Self-employed individuals can apply for Royal Enfield finance. You will need to provide your last 2 years' Income Tax Returns (ITR), a 6-month bank statement, and business registration documents in addition to standard KYC documents.
Is there a prepayment penalty? +
Prepayment terms vary by lending partner. Several of our banking partners — including HDFC and ICICI — offer zero foreclosure charges after a lock-in period of typically 6–12 months. Please confirm the specific terms with your dealership finance executive at the time of loan booking.
